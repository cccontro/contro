    R1back to ToC or SWRL ToC

    An argument that concludes B from A has the implicit premise that there exists an implication from A to B

    Premise op(?arg,?prem) , Conclusion op(?arg,?conc) , Antecedent op(?inf,?prem) , Consequent op(?inf,?conc) -> Premise op(?arg,?inf)

    op: Object Properties
